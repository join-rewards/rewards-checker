Frequently Asked Questions

What is a wallet rewards checker?

A wallet rewards checker scans your crypto wallet address across multiple blockchains to find unclaimed airdrops, staking rewards, governance tokens, and other assets you might not know you own.

Is this service free to use?

Yes, our wallet scanner is completely free to use. We provide this as a service to the crypto community to help users discover value they may have overlooked.

How do I check for unclaimed crypto rewards?

Simply enter your wallet address in the scanner above, click "Scan Wallet Now," and our system will automatically check for all types of unclaimed rewards across multiple protocols and blockchains.

What types of rewards can I find?

Our scanner can find unclaimed airdrops, staking rewards, liquidity mining rewards, governance tokens, retroactive rewards, points, and potentially dangerous token approvals.
